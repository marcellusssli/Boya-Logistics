About Boya Logistics

À propos de Boya Logistics

Boya Logistics 是一家立足加拿大、服务全球的现代化国际物流企业，专注于为跨境贸易、电商企业、批发商及品牌客户提供专业、高效、可靠的一站式供应链解决方案。

Boya Logistics is a Canadian-based international logistics company serving global trade, e-commerce businesses, wholesalers, and brands with professional, efficient, and reliable supply chain solutions.

Boya Logistics est une entreprise canadienne de logistique internationale offrant aux entreprises de commerce mondial, e-commerce, grossistes et marques des solutions fiables et efficaces.
